Trying to find Quakers near you? Try one of these links:

General

  • Australia
  • Canada
  • Quaker Maps: Canada, UK, US
  • United Kingdom
  • United States

Note that the “worldwide” link also includes a way to register yourself as an “isolated Friend” if there’s no meeting near you. With time, new meetings can form.

LGBTQ+

If you are concerned about LGBTQ+ affirmation, here are lists of meetings that have formally stated their affirming position.

  • Same-gender marriage list
  • Transgender list

Notes

  1. Please note that sometimes you’ll see a local meeting’s name, and sometimes you’ll see an association of meetings (such as a yearly meeting) listed. In the latter case, all meetings that belong to it are covered.
  2. Any declaration that combined marriage and transgender concerns into one document was put into the first list and left out of the second. That means the true number of trans affirming meetings is much longer, so be sure to check both lists!
