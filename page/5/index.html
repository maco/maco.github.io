A Vision of Racism and the Cross

Mackenzie Morgan published on 2020-06-07 included in Religion
Background This message was shared at West Hills Friends Church on 7 June 2020, as protests continued following the killing of George Floyd by police. It was presented over Zoom, due to the COVID-19 pandemic. This traveling ministry was undertaken under the care of Baltimore Yearly Meeting of the Religious Society of Friends. Scripture A reading from the Gospel of Matthew, chapter 25, verses 31 through 46. “When the Son of Man comes in his glory, and all the angels with him, then he will sit on the throne of his glory.
